Dinning Halls

Campus East and Campus West restaurants are the community favorite dining halls with rotating menus and multiple counter themes serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. This is the place to sample global favorites, including Arabic cuisines, stone-baked pizzas, grilled fish & meats, hot Asian inspired dishes, dedicated vegan and vegetarian counter, and a large self-serve salad bar with over 30 items to choose from. Accepts meal plan.
